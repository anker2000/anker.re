Base Notes

The base notes of Sandalwood, Cedarwood, Guaiac Wood, Patchouli, Erogen Musk and, most interestingly, Oak Moss add depth and complexity, making it an extraordinarily compelling perfume.

Heart Notes

Most interesting of all is Clary Sage, the sacred herb that Emperor Nero used to sprinkle over his troops before they went into battle, so invigorating and energizing did he deem it to be. Clary Sage reminds us of Neroli, Lavender, Musk and Ambergris.
